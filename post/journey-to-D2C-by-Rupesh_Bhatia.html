   Functional  -- Consultant

If you are under path of Journey to CTA. you have may aware that Consulting mindset is required in order to achieve CTA ( Certified Technical Architect).
We have more Architects, Solution Architects, Technical Architects, Developers compare with Consultant, Admin, Business Analyst in our current market and Huge scope of Consultant in current market.

What is Consulting ??
Consulting is Art of listening. A Consultant need to have capabilities to analysis the problem with his analytical thinking and Communicate solution with his technical expertise.

What are skills companies looking for ??
   He/ She need to have capability to handle and Communication with clients
   Who can help to resolve their problem as a team player/ Individual by showing his/her technical capabilities
   Capable of ready to travel anywhere whenever it is required

What are skills clients looking for ??
   He/ She need to be a Techno-Functional has capabilities to guide Business/ IT teams
   who can suggest resolutions with best practices within Salesforce Ecosystem
   Need to be a Technical Advisor who has capabilities to build Quick POC/ Alternative solution with approach

Want to be Consultant ??

add below skills into your bucket

    Learn depth Understand of your business implementation
    How to make feasible solution with OOTB in salesforce Ecosystem
    Update technical with every release of salesforce and make update your skills
    Try to provide solutions inputs with implementation capabilities

Onshore Responsibilities

OnShore Consultant deals with multiple roles in their day to day such as Consultant, Business Analyst and many more roles. To get analysis of gathering requirements from clients and provide best solutions for implementation and work closely associated with OffShore dev teams.

OffShore Responsibilities

OffShore teams works on tasks assigned by Onshore team and need to understand business requirements and provide accurate solutions which reduces users problems. Majorly concentrating on covering sprint release.

PreSales

It is a process or a set of activities/sales normally carried out before a customer is acquired, though sometimes presales also extends into the period the product or service is delivered to the customer.

Project Execution

Sprint Planning vs Change Management

UAT training

Provide UAT user training in a simplest form. Reslove doubts of users and get feedback for users. Implement suggestion by user and add their suggest into next release to make smooth transaction to users.

Effective Communication

Communication can't become a barrier to get requirements from your stakeholders/ clients. Invest time wisely to your soft skills

Call for Action

Learn - Reflect - Invest

    Find a mentor on your workplace/ community
    Learn to ASK why I am building it.
    Learn say to NO supported by right amount of technical knowledge
    Have a patience focus on Long term vs Short term goals
    Develop your soft skills by learn call/ email etiquette
    Reflect your decisions and working on build your skills
    Invest your time on your career path
